Our Story

There is a rising trend of people seeking emotional support through public disclosures on social networks. However, the outcomes are mixed, with some receiving minimal encouragement while others face backlash or indifference which can worsen their emotional distress.

We believe that everyone deserves to have access to mental health support, and we wanted to create a space where people could connect with others who understood what they were going through.

Solution

Leeway is a safe and supportive space where people can talk about their mental health without judgment. We offer a variety of features to help people connect with others, including:

Discussion Forum

Share your experiences and connect with others who understand what you're going through.

One-on-one chat

Talk to a counselor or therapist about your mental health.

Support Group

Find a group of people who are going through the same thing as you.

Resources

Find information and resources about mental health.
